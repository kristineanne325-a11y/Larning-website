About Wildlife Guardians

About Us

Wildlife Guardians is a nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting wildlife and their habitats through conservation, research, and education. We work with local communities to create sustainable solutions that benefit both people and nature.

Since our founding in 2010, we've run habitat restoration projects, species monitoring programs, rescue and rehabilitation efforts, and community outreach to build long-term stewardship. You can help by volunteering, donating, or spreading the word.
